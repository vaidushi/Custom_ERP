Administrative-structure

eMAPS acts as the administrative heart of your business, defining your company structure, roles, and access rights. This granular control ensures only authorized personnel manage specific functions.

Budget Management

The ERP's bank interface streamlines budgeting. Create detailed plans linked to your accounting system for clear tracking. Define separate budgets for operational costs and capital expenditures.

Bank Interface

eMAPS simplifies finances. It integrates with your bank for automated vendor onboarding via Digital Signature Certificates (DSC) and Public Financial Management System (PFMS) authentication.

Financial Management

The ERP's Financial Management module empowers control. Create a general ledger linked to budgets for real-time tracking.

Flow of Funds

eMAPS keeps your cash flow in check. Book liabilities for upcoming financial commitments. Offices submit electronic fund requests mapped to budgets, with built-in approval workflows for secure disbursement.

HRMS

eMAPS simplifies HR. Automate payroll for on-time, accurate payments. Manage employee loans directly in the system for a smoother application and repayment process. Streamline attendance, leave, and recruitment with real-time insights and applicant tracking tools.
