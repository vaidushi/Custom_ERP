The ERP's bank interface streamlines budgeting. Create detailed plans linked to your accounting system for clear tracking. Bank Interface

eMAPS simplifies finances. It integrates with your bank for automated vendor onboarding via Digital Signature Certificates (DSC) and Public Financial Management System (PFMS) authentication. Financial Management

The ERP's Financial Management module empowers control. Create a general ledger linked to budgets for real-time tracking. Seamless bank integration automates reconciliation and minimizes errors. Manage the entire purchase-to-pay and order-to-cash cycle, streamlining financial processes.

The system handles payments, capturing details like UTR numbers for transparent and auditable transactions

HRMS

eMAPS simplifies HR. Automate payroll for on-time, accurate payments. Manage employee loans directly in the system for a smoother application and repayment process. Streamline attendance, leave, and recruitment with real-time insights and applicant tracking tools.
